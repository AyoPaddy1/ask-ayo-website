📖 The Definition

EBITDA

"Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization. A measure of a company's operating performance that excludes the effects of financing and accounting decisions."

💬 Real Talk

EBITDA

"It's basically how much money a company makes from its actual business, before you factor in loans, taxes, and accounting stuff. Think of it as: 'How good is this company at what it actually does?'"

We keep definitions faithful to common finance sources, and Real Talk focused on what it means for you.
